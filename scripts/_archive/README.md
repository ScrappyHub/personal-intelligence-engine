\# PIE Script Archive



This directory preserves historical repair and bootstrap evidence.



\## Purpose



These files are retained as archaeological evidence of deterministic repair work that led to the first Tier-0 FULL\_GREEN state.



They are \*\*not\*\* the authoritative product surface.



\## Authoritative product surface



The authoritative product scripts are the ones referenced by:



\- `README.md`

\- `SPEC.md`

\- `LAW.md`

\- `freeze\\PIE\_TIER0\_FREEZE\_MANIFEST\_v1.txt`



\## Archive categories



\- `repairs/` — patch and fix scripts used during recovery

\- `backups/` — `.bak\_\*` and overwritten prior states

\- `transcripts/` — historical outputs and debugging traces

\- `bootstrap/` — temporary scaffold writers and transitional runners



\## Rule



Nothing in `\_archive/` is authoritative unless explicitly promoted later.
